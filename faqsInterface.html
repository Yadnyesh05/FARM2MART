FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION's

1. What is Farm2Market?

Farm2Market is a web-based platform that provides real-time market intelligence and direct market access for farmers, eliminating middlemen and ensuring fair trade.

2. Who can use Farm2Market?

Farmers, wholesalers, retailers, food processing companies, restaurants, and logistics providers can use the platform.

3. How does Farm2Market benefit farmers?

It provides real-time price updates, direct buyer connections, and a bidding system, ensuring better profits and fair trade.

4. Is Farm2Market free for farmers?

Yes, the basic functionalities are free for farmers, but there may be premium features for added benefits.

5. How can farmers list their produce?

Farmers can register on the platform, enter product details (quantity, price, location), and publish their listings.

6. How do buyers place requests?

Buyers can register, post their requirements, and view farmer listings to make direct purchases or place bids.

7. Can farmers negotiate directly with buyers?

Yes, the platform facilitates direct communication and negotiation between farmers and buyers.

8. What technologies power Farm2Market?

The platform uses React, Java, Spring Boot, MySQL/MongoDB, RESTful APIs, and is hosted on AWS/Firebase/Vercel.

10. Is my data safe on Farm2Market?

Yes, the platform follows industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect user data.

11. Do I need to share personal details to register?

Only basic details like name, location, and contact information are required for authentication and trade transparency.

12. Will Farm2Market expand to more regions?

Yes, future plans include expanding to more agricultural markets and integrating with government price data.
